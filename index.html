OUR SERVICES


Our core business as a provider of Marine, Land and Aerial surveying technology focus on on the geospatial field, monitoring solutions and IT solutions.

Marine

Land

Aerial

Provides various services in the field of geospatial surveys and mapping, including aerial surveys using drones, land and building measurements, hydrographic survey, area mapping and digital maps, spatial data analysis, and so on. In addition, this company also provides consulting and solutions in the use of geospatial technology, development of geographic information systems, and training in geospatial and mapping fields.

PT Georama Karya Indonesia has high standards in service quality and work safety. Our company ensures that all services provided to clients comply with international standards and regulations in Indonesia. In addition, we also has certified ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018.
